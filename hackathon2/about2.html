Who we Are?

Aishwi Technologies PVT LTD was founded on March 24, 2021, and is primarily focused on artificial intelligence. We have undertaken a project called UHID, an app for patient health records based on AI. Additionally, we have supported a project called Swayam for the Union Ministry of Education. Our company has developed a separate set of tools, including accounting tools based on AI. These tools are designed for ZOHO to facilitate ledger and account book management with the assistance of AI, reducing the need for extensive human workforce. Furthermore, we have collaborated with Company Netenrich Technologies on the Swayam 2.0 project, aimed at providing education support for underprivileged individuals. This collaboration was conducted in partnership with the Union Ministry of Education and is considered one of the most prestigious projects for our company. On January 7, 2024. We have accounting software called Ankapal, used for creating invoices and facilitating direct GST returns through websites. Ankapal employs AI for ledger creation, helping eliminate fake invoices.
